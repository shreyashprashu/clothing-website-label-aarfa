Refund, Exchange & Cancellation Policy

Refunds

We do not offer refunds or returns once an order is placed.

Damaged, defective, or incorrect items

If your parcel arrives damaged, defective, or contains an incorrect product, share a complete unboxing video of the parcel — without any cuts or edits — within 24 hours of delivery. Claims without an unboxing video will not be accepted. Once the item reaches our warehouse, it undergoes a one-day quality check. After pickup, the replacement typically takes 10 to 15 days to reach you.

How exchanges work

In place of a refund, you may choose any other dress of equivalent value in exchange for the one you received. We do not process refund requests in cash — for prepaid orders, the full amount of the dress (excluding courier charges) is issued as a coupon code or applied to an exchange dress, depending on the order value.

Cancellation

Orders cannot be cancelled once successfully placed.

Dispatch & delivery

Ready-stock orders are usually dispatched within 2 working days. Delivery typically takes 7–8 business days, depending on the destination. Shipping details are shared via email once the order is dispatched.
